Privacy Agreement for Mindful Waters Foundation

1. Introduction

At Mindful Waters Foundation, we are deeply committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services, including our online form for requesting professional mental health support.

This policy is intended to be transparent and informative, so you understand how we treat your data. For any questions or further clarification, please reach out to us at privacy@mindfulwatersfoundation.org.

2. Information Collection

Types of Information Collected

We may collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and other details provided when filling out our online request form or contacting us.
  • Non-Personal Information: Information about your usage of our website, such as browsing behavior, pages visited, and interactions.

Collection Methods

Direct Collection: We collect information that you provide directly through form submissions, email communications, or other direct interactions with our website.

Automatic Collection: Certain information, such as IP addresses, browser type, and device information, may be collected automatically as you interact with our site.

3. Use of Collected Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide services: Fulfilling requests for information or professional support as requested via our online form.
  • To improve services: Enhancing website functionality, user experience, and resources available through Mindful Waters Foundation.
  • To communicate with you: Responding to inquiries, providing updates, or sharing resources related to our mission.

Legal Bases for Processing: We process data on the basis of user consent, contractual necessity (where applicable), and legitimate interest in providing effective mental health support resources. Where legally required, we will obtain explicit consent for data processing.

4. Sharing of Information

Third-Party Sharing

We may share personal information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our activities, or delivering services. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any purposes other than those specified.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, personal information may be transferred as part of our business assets, in compliance with applicable laws.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or to comply with legal processes, protect the rights or property of Mindful Waters Foundation, or safeguard the personal safety of our users.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect non-personal information and improve site functionality.

Purpose of Cookies

Cookies help us analyze website traffic, enhance user experience, and ensure that our services remain secure and effective.

User Choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, and you may choose to disable cookies if desired. Note that some site features may not function optimally without cookies.

6. Data Storage and Security

Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. Our measures include encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews.

International Data Transfers

If any data is transferred internationally, it will be handled in compliance with relevant data protection laws to ensure appropriate safeguards.

7. User Rights and Choices

Access, Correction, and Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Please contact us at privacy@mindfulwatersfoundation.org to make such requests.

Data Portability and Restriction Rights

Where applicable, you may have rights to request data portability or restrict certain data processing activities.

Opt-Out Options

You can opt-out of certain communications, such as marketing emails, by following unsubscribe instructions or contacting us directly.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child’s information has been collected without parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or relevant laws. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
